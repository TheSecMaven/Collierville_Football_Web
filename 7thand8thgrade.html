About 7th/8th Grade Collierville Football



Thank you for your interest in Collierville Dragon Football. There will be two combined 7/8th Grade teams this season. The Head Coaches will be Dan Holcomb and Jeff Bousson. All athletes will practice together the first week for evaluation. At the end of the week, they will be placed on the maroon or the white team. We will split teams as evenly as possible. Each combo team will consist of a Varsity (all 8th graders and some 7th) and a JV team (7th grade only). Each team will play two games a night, a Varsity game followed by a JV game. Our two teams will not play each other in the regular season. We will play other teams with the same make up of players. The following are important dates and information.

The club only supplies home and away jerseys you have to purchase the rest. This includes:
  1. White helmet with grey facemask
  2. White practice jersey
  3. White game pants with Pads
  4. Shoulder Pads
  1. Mouth Piece
  2. White practice pants with pads
  3. Cleats (any color)
  4. Athletic Support/cup (optional)

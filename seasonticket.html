Collierville High School Football Season Tickets

Hello Dragon Families!



2019 CHS Dragon Football Season Tickets and General Admission Passes Now open for purchase!

Dragons Season Tickets Registration and Payment Site

When the Season Ticket screen opens you will find links at the top that will show you the seating charts. There are 2 seating charts --one for seat back chairs and one for reserved bleacher seats. The seat back chairs are $125 and the reserved bleachers are $75. Please indicate your first and second choice seats on the registration form. Please remember that these seats are ONLY being reserved on a first come first serve basis when payment is made online. We will do our best to seat you at, or near the seats you request. You also have the option of purchasing general admission season passes for entrance to ALL home games for Varsity, JV, Freshmen and Middle School for $50 (excludes any potential playoff games and the jamboree). If you have a problem with making the payment online please let us know.

If you have any questions or problems using the system please let us know and we will be glad to help in any way we can. GO DRAGONS!!

Please contact me, Amy Wilhelm, at chs.dragons.tickets@gmail.com.
